Available Services

The following services are available on this server:

  • Plex: Stream movies and TV shows
  • Overseerr: Request new movies and TV shows
  • Audiobookshelf: Listen to audiobooks and podcasts
  • Kavita: Read e-books and comics


Getting Access

Follow these steps to access each service:


Plex & Overseerr

  1. Create an account at plex.tv
  2. Send me your Plex email address to receive an invitation
  3. Accept the invitation via the email from Plex
  4. Access content at app.plex.tv
  5. Use your Plex account to log in to Overseerr

Audiobookshelf

  1. Request access from me
  2. Receive your temporary login credentials
  3. Log in at audiobookshelf.owens-services.xyz
  4. Change your password after first login

Kavita

  1. Request access from me
  2. Receive your temporary login credentials
  3. Log in at kavita.owens-services.xyz
  4. Change your password after first login


Requesting Content

To request new movies or TV shows:

  1. Log in to Overseerr with your Plex account
  2. Search for the content you want
  3. Click the "Request" button

Important Notes:

  • For TV shows, please request only 1-2 seasons at a time
  • Most content will download automatically
  • Older or less popular content may require manual processing


Reporting Issues

If you encounter problems with any content:

  1. Report issues through Overseerr, not Plex
  2. Provide specific details about the problem
  3. For playback issues, try downloading the content first

I'll address reported issues as quickly as possible.
